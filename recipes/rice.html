Guela's Spanish Rice

Home

No home cooked meal is complete without a side of spanish rice!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup rice
  • 2 Cups BOILING water
  • Approx. 1/3 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 Onion diced
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic minced
  • 1/2 Cup of bell pepper (Green, Red, Yellow), diced
  • 1/2 t. Ground cumin
  • 1/2 t. Oregano
  • 1/2 t. Salt
  • 1/2 t. Pepper

Directions:

  1. Get all ingredients ready because prep goes fast!
  2. Start boiling 3-4 cups of water. You only need 2 cups but some will evaporate.
  3. Heat 1 T of oil in your skillet. (See meat option)
  4. When oil is hot, add rice and stir until lightly toasted, 1-2 minutes. Add onion, garlic and bell peppers. Guela used to add the bell peppers in big pieces when we were children so she could take them out but still add the flavor. You can add them diced. :)
  5. Add salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.

    6. Optional: If you want to add meat, add small pieces of beef or chicken. Brown in hot oil. Use low heat so meat cooks thoroughly. Add rice and continue with step 4.

  6. Add the tomato sauce; stir.
  7. Add the hot water; stir and taste the liquid, salt if needed.
  8. Bring to boil then lower heat to simmer. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes; do not lift lid.
  9. After 15 minutes, remove from heat; do not lift cover; let heat/steam continue to cook the rice.
